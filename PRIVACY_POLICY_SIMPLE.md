# Privacy Policy for Smart Community Mobile App

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025

## About This App

**Smart Community** is a mobile application that helps students and parents manage school meals, wallet balances, and family profiles. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Details:** Your name, email address, and phone number
- **School Information:** School ID and grade level
- **Family Data:** Information about family members you manage
- **Payment Information:** Wallet balance and transaction history
- **Meal Preferences:** Your food choices and dietary restrictions

### Technical Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, and app version
- **Usage Data:** How you use the app (features used, time spent)
- **Location Data:** General location for school verification (if enabled)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide Services:** Process meal orders and manage your wallet
- **Account Management:** Create and maintain your user account
- **Communication:** Send you notifications about orders and updates
- **Support:** Help you with technical issues and questions
- **Improvement:** Make the app better based on how it's used
- **Security:** Protect your account from unauthorized access

## Information Sharing

### We Do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your information for marketing purposes outside our app

### We DO Share:
- **With Your School:** Meal orders and payment information for school records
- **With Service Providers:** Payment processors and notification services (under strict agreements)
- **When Required by Law:** If legally required or to protect rights and safety

## Data Security

We protect your information using:
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted when transmitted and stored
- **Access Controls:** Only authorized personnel can access your data
- **Regular Security Updates:** We continuously update our security measures
- **Secure Servers:** Your data is stored on secure, monitored servers

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** See what information we have about you
- **Update:** Correct or update your information
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Opt-out:** Stop receiving notifications
- **Export:** Get a copy of your data

## Children's Privacy

- **Minimum Age:** Users must be at least 13 years old
- **Parental Consent:** Required for users under 18
- **School Verification:** We verify school email addresses
- **No Collection:** We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13

## Data Retention

We keep your information for:
- **Account Data:** Until you delete your account
- **Transaction Records:** 7 years (for legal and tax purposes)
- **Usage Analytics:** 2 years (in anonymous form)
- **Support Logs:** 90 days

## International Users

This app complies with:
- **GDPR:** European Union privacy regulations
- **CCPA:** California Consumer Privacy Act
- **Local Laws:** Privacy laws in your country

## Changes to This Policy

- We will notify you of any significant changes
- Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy
- Previous versions are available upon request
